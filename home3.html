This is a chart of Humidity versus City Latitude. Note there were no significant discernible patterns, however there was some general indication that lower humidities are more likely to be encountered in cities that are at plus and minus 20 degrees from the equator. with a higher frequency of lower humidities in the northern hemisphere i.e. plus 20 and greater degrees latitude.
